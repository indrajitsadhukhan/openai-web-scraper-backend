PwC is a lifelong experience
At PwC, we are driven by our people, our employees whose dedication and commitment sets us apart and those who have over the years built a foundation on which we stand today – our alumni. We welcome you to join this family of thousands of alumni, stay connected with the firm and with one another, share your success stories and memories, strengthen your network and make meaningful relationships.
We value your association with PwC, even after you leave the firm. The PwC India Alumni network helps you stay connected with your former colleagues and friends who have been an integral part of your journey here. If you have been a part of the PwC network of firms in India or have worked in any PwC firm and are now living in India, we invite you to join our programme. You can join the PwC lndia Alumni network by registering yourself on our Alumni portal.
To connect, write to us -- alumni.pwcindia@in.pwc.com
