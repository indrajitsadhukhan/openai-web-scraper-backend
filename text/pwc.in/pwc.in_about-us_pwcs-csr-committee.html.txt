Arnab Basu, Advisory Leader, PwC India
Arnab joined PwC post his MBA from Indian Institute of Management, Calcutta in 2001. In his previous role, he was the Technology Consulting leader for PwC India for the past five years. He is also the Regional Managing Partner, East region and has expanded our footprints across the region including Bangladesh, Nepal and Bhutan.  He has extensive consulting experience nationally and internationally in diverse fields like Digital Strategy & Business Transformation, Financial Performance Management, Removing Complexity, and defining the Digital Journey leveraging Enterprise Applications. He is part of our Global Technology Leadership team and has worked for a range of top tier clients across multiple industries including Industrial Products, Discrete Manufacturing, Metals & Mining, Oil & Gas.

Bimal Tanna, Partner Affairs Leader, PwC India
Bimal is the Partner Affairs Leader of PwC India. He is part of the India Leadership Team.
In the two decades with PwC, Bimal played several leadership and client-facing roles, including India West Region Managing Partner, Industrial Products Sector Leader, Deputy Deals Leader, Valuations Leader, Private Equity Leader, India West Region Deals Leader, HC Leader for Assurance. Prior to PwC, Bimal continues to be the Global Relationship Partner for a very large Indian conglomerate which is an India Priority Account.

The experience encompasses a broad range of industries including industrial manufacturing, auto and auto components, aerospace and defence, chemicals, pharmaceuticals, real estate and infrastructure, oil and gas, healthcare, financial services, hospitality, information technology, media and telecom, travel and tourism.
Bimal is a fellow member of the Institute of Chartered Accountants of India (ICAI).
