Information for potential and existing suppliers to PwC India
Thank you for your interest in becoming a supplier of products and/or services.






By clicking on the “Access the portal" button below, you will be redirected to “PwC's Supplier Engagement Hub” (the “Portal”). In the Portal, you will be asked to submit details about your organisation that will help us evaluate your products and services. Post approval, you would also be allowed to submit government approved digitally signed invoices, track status, download TDS certificates, etc. using the portal.
Accessing the Portal is free, easy and only takes a few minutes. Once you select the “Access the portal" button, you will be routed to a secure website to Login/complete the registration process. This supplier portal will allow you to provide and maintain additional details about your organisation and submit invoices.




PwC’s procurement and finance professionals will review your registration request, invoices submitted and would approve them before accepting any liability. PwC will contact you if there is any additional information required in respect of your organisation’s capabilities or legal documents or your invoice.
Please note that your completion of the registration process and submission of invoice (i) Does not guarantee that any current or future business will be awarded to your organisation by PwC, and (ii) imposes no legal obligations on PwC to accept any liability
PwC refers to one or more members firms in India of PwC Network, each of which is a separate legal entity. Please see www.pwc.com/structure for further details.
For any login/access issues, please reach out to ithelpdek.vms@in.pwc.com and/or +91 124 627 7510
