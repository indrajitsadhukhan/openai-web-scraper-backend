Loan Origination System



Automated solution for faster loan processing.
The Venerate Lending Solution (VLS) is Venerate’s Loan Origination System (LOS) accelerator. It’s an end-to-end solution for the loan application process assisted journey, and all loan origination needs – that can further be customised for unique business processes.
VLS accelerates the loan origination process; it automates, digitises, and standardises workflows to ensure a seamless experience in the application process through to approval stage. This includes a credit check and softcopy approval, verification process, UW/CC approval, and regulatory compliance check.
 









EmployAble



Digital solutions to refocus, retrain and reskill for today's job market.
Intelligent Job Matching capabilities identify and select appropriate opportunities based on skill, location and experience. The solution aggregates and verifies hundreds of thousands of live job listings from a multitude of job portals, directories, corporate and government sites for a centralised, deduplicated repository which is intelligently matched to candidates.









Marketplace Solution



A curated offering based on specific requirements of each buyer matching requirements to offerings.
Venerate’s Marketplace Solution helps to manage all the stakeholders involved in the business process into a single marketplace, with role-based security that you can rely on.
The entire lifecycle from the generation of requirements of various types of entities to fulfilment, invoicing and payments are captured in the solution.
Marketplace Solution offers multiple validation stages, with advanced verification processes, to help authenticate stakeholders and secure the business operations. This is a mobile ready solution which can be accessed with the Salesforce mobile app.
Marketplace Solution features:

Capturing requirements from the buyer, and matching them with the available products and service offerings.
Approvals and workflows, if any, involved in the business process.
Activating the supply chain for fulfilment and acceptance of payment milestones.
Proof of delivery of the product/services, and feedback surveys.
Invoicing and validation of invoices and trigger payments; Receipts of final payments.


 









Data Archiving



Data archiving solution for timely, business critical reporting and analysis
The amount of data being created and stored is growing more rapidly than ever before, with experts predicting a 20-fold increase in the next 10 years.
While much of this data can be tremendously useful to companies seeking to understand their customers, large volumes of data place a huge strain on business applications. This can cause performance issues in loading, filtering and indexing huge quantities of data, and impact a company’s ability to serve its customers quickly and efficiently.
Venerate’s Salesforce data archiving solution is Based on AWS, the data archiving solution meets these challenges head on to produce and deliver timely, business critical reporting and analysis.
